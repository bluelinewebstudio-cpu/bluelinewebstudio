Lakeview Dental Group: booking made simple across five locations

Five offices, five different scheduling quirks, and a patient base that expected to book online at 11pm. Lakeview needed one clean system that worked everywhere.

Client
Lakeview Dental Group
Industry
Healthcare
Timeline
9 weeks
Services
Design, Dev, SEO

The challenge

Each of Lakeview's five locations ran its own booking process — two used a scheduling app, three took requests by phone. Patients couldn't tell which office was closest or see real availability, and front-desk staff spent hours a day just confirming appointments.

What we built

We designed a single, calm, trustworthy site with a location-aware booking flow: patients pick a service, see the nearest office with real openings, and confirm in under two minutes. Staff manage every location from one dashboard.

  • Unified booking flow across all five locations
  • Location finder with real-time availability
  • New patient intake forms completed before the visit
  • Local SEO overhaul so each office ranks in its own neighborhood

The result

Online bookings now make up the majority of new appointments, front-desk call volume dropped by a third, and each location shows up in local search results for its own neighborhood instead of competing with the others.
