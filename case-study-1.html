Windy City Roasters: a storefront that keeps up with demand

A West Loop coffee roaster needed an online shop that could handle subscriptions, wholesale orders, and a growing retail line — without the checkout friction of their old platform.

Client
Windy City Roasters
Industry
Food & Beverage
Timeline
7 weeks
Services
Design, Dev, E-Commerce

The challenge

Windy City Roasters had outgrown their off-the-shelf storefront. Subscription management was clunky, page loads dragged past six seconds on mobile, and wholesale customers had no way to place recurring orders without emailing the shop directly.

What we built

We designed a warm, editorial storefront that puts the product and story front and center, then built it on a fast, custom checkout flow with three order types: one-time purchase, recurring subscription, and wholesale accounts with net-30 terms.

  • Custom subscription builder with flexible cadence and roast preferences
  • Wholesale portal with saved order templates and invoicing
  • Sub-2 second load times across product and category pages
  • Integrated local pickup scheduling for the West Loop roastery

The result

Within the first month, online orders rose 40% and subscription sign-ups climbed steadily as customers found the new builder easier to use than the old plan picker. Wholesale accounts now place orders directly through the portal, cutting order-processing time for the Windy City team by hours each week.
