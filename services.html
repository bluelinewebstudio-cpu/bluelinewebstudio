What we do

Everything your website needs, under one roof.

From the first sketch to the ten-thousandth visitor, our studio handles strategy, design, code, content, and the maintenance that keeps it all running.

01 · Web Design

Design that looks like your business, not a template.

We start with your customers and your goals, then design custom layouts, type, and color around them — mobile-first, accessible, and unmistakably yours.

  • Custom UI design in Figma, reviewed with you at every stage
  • Mobile-first responsive layouts for every screen size
  • Accessible color, type, and interaction patterns (WCAG AA)
02 · Development

Hand-coded, fast, and built to last.

No bloated site-builder plugins. We write clean, modern code that loads in seconds and stays easy to maintain as your business grows.

  • Sub-2.5s load times, optimized images and assets
  • Clean, documented code your next developer can read
  • Built on modern, well-supported frameworks
03 · E-Commerce

Storefronts that turn browsers into buyers.

Smooth checkout, real inventory management, and product pages designed to sell — whether you run 10 SKUs or 10,000.

  • Secure checkout with major payment providers
  • Inventory, shipping, and order management tools
  • Conversion-focused product and cart pages
04 · SEO & Growth

Built to be found, not just built to look good.

Technical SEO, structured content, and local search optimization so Chicago customers searching for what you do actually land on your site.

  • On-page SEO, sitemap, and schema markup
  • Local search & Google Business Profile setup
  • Monthly performance & ranking reports
05 · Branding

A visual identity that holds up everywhere.

Logo, color palette, typography, and voice guidelines — consistent across your site, socials, and print.

  • Logo design with editable source files
  • Color, type, and imagery style guide
  • Brand voice & messaging guidelines
06 · Care & Maintenance

Your site, always current — without lifting a finger.

Security updates, backups, uptime monitoring, and small content edits, handled proactively every month.

  • Automatic backups & uptime monitoring
  • Monthly content updates included
  • Priority support, real humans, same-day replies
